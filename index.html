Good to know

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How much does window cleaning cost?

It depends on the number of windows, stories, and whether you want interior too — most homes fall in a predictable range. Send a quick quote request and we'll give you an exact, no-obligation price the same day.

Do I need to be home?

For exterior-only cleans, no — as long as we have gate access and water. For interior work we'll schedule a time that works for you.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

We back every visit with a streak-free guarantee. If a pane isn't perfect, we'll come back and redo it — no questions, no charge.

How often should I get my windows cleaned?

Any clean beats no clean — but the more often, the better your glass looks year-round. Here's the quick guide for Utah homes:

  • Once a year Good
  • Quarterly Better
  • Every other month Awesome
  • Monthly ★ Best
